About Me

I am a Toronto based UX designer who is working on creating marvelous feature-rich high fidelity designs. I believe that nothing thrives unless it simplifies human effort, and only the user holds the reigns to that verdict. That’s where my design realm exists: human-centric and user-driven. And there lies my goal─ to design for my users intuitively and interactively. From the inception of my concept, I realized that design was my calling. That is the prime reason why I put my heart and soul into each new project, and this intense passion for user-centric design is reflected in myhighly varied portfolio which I try to make exceptional.
